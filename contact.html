Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions I receive from clients.

I specialize in cinematic video editing, motion graphics, and creative design. My projects range from commercial videos, social media content, YouTube videos, to documentary-style edits and brand storytelling.

Turnaround time depends on project complexity. A standard 1-2 minute video typically takes 3-5 business days, while more complex projects with motion graphics may take 1-2 weeks. Rush services are available for an additional fee.

I include 2 rounds of revisions in my standard pricing to ensure you're completely satisfied. Additional revisions can be arranged for an extra fee. Clear communication upfront helps minimize the need for extensive revisions.

Final deliverables are typically provided as MP4 files in H.264 codec (for web use) or ProRes (for broadcast quality). I can provide other formats based on your specific needs, including GIFs for social media snippets.
